Chemical Engineering

The Federal University of Technology Owerri — widely known as FUTO — is one of Nigeria's elite technical universities, established with a singular focus: producing world-class engineers and scientists. Located in Owerri, the capital of Imo State, FUTO has for decades been the training ground for Nigeria's most brilliant technical minds.

The Chemical Engineering programme at FUTO is rigorous by design, blending theoretical depth with practical application. From foundational sciences through to advanced process engineering, the curriculum equips graduates with the capability to innovate across Nigeria's key industries — oil & gas, petrochemicals, food processing, water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and sustainable energy.
